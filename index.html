About Me

I am an imaginative, resourceful and strategic problem-solver looking to get back to my roots as a software engineer. For the past 12 years, I have been a product owner / principal business analyst supporting agile teams as the conduit between the team and our customers.

In my spare time, I love to read, watch rom-coms and be crafty through knitting or quilting. I also enjoy listening to music - everything from hiphop to country to grunge. I love to be outside and am an avid fisherperson. Fun fact: I hold the Annual Father and Son Fishing Trip Record for the largest walleye.

My other full-time job and joy is to be mom to my kiddos and puppy. I have a daughter, Anita, who is 6 and a son, Rudy, who is 2. My dog, Belley Sue, is a black and tan coonhound/lab mix and she is about 15!
